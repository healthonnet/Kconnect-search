Trust what you read on the Web – Background and Disclaimer

The general objectives of the Web browser extension are to propose a tool to access trustworthy health information online.

The HONcode, the quality criteria defined for health and medical Web publishers by the Health On the Net (HON) Foundation is a set of ethical, honesty, transparency and quality standards covering various aspects of health website content production. HONcode certification process is a manual process conducted by HON team members assessing health website according to the HONcode. The HONcode is the most used model for the identification of health sites that are trustworthy and respect quality criteria.

The HONcode certification only covers a limited portion of the Health Web so within the KHRESMOI.eu, and Kconnect.eu European projects, HON investigated different approaches to improve access to quality health information online.

Three approaches are addressed: query formulation, readability of health documents and trust level. The research focuses on the use of intelligent information access (IIA) to promote quality health websites by developing computer algorithms that automatically identify the presence of HONcode principles on a site.

The trust level approach has concentrated the major research activities on benchmarking and assessing natural language processing (NLP) methods for the automated detection of HONcode principles. Systematic evaluation, and usability testing have been conducted to assess the validity of the automatic methods and have been publish in European deliverables and scientific publications listed below. In general the automated detection of the presence of HONcode principles is producing very good results. Please note that the trust and readability scores are defined by an automated system so in some cases it can be deficient or not optimum.

The Khresmoi project developed a multilingual, multimodal search and access system for medical and health information and documents in order to address the challenges of retrieving the relevant health information located in huge amounts of medical data. Khresmoi for Everyone offers HONcode certified websites and a curated list of online health resources that are manually checked to ensure that they meet the HONcode criteria for health information online. It aims to facilitate access to high-quality health information for Internet users and, through this, fulfill the right to information and health. Within this project, HON developed the health search engine and started research related to the automated detection of HONcode principles.

HON is finalizing the research related to the automated detection of HONcode principle as well as finding solutions to integrate it into easy-to-use tools. HON is also in charge of developing services based on classification to guide the end user toward health content sorted according to the complexity of the content and to assess health websites according to the HONcode principles. HON is involved in providing users with a semantic search adapted to their needs.
